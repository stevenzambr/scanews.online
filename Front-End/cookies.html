Cookies

Scanews uses Cookies to save configured settings (locally on the device accessing the website).
If Cookies have previously been enabled for Scanews, the Disable Cookies button below will disable them again. If Cookies are disabled the set settings are not saved, on the next reload of Scanews the default settings will load again.

Cookies can be enabled by clicking the blue I agree button on the pop-up message that appears at bottom of the page. The pop-up message only appears if Cookies are not enabled. The pop-up message can be dismissed by clicking the I disagree Button, keep it mind that if Cookies are not enabled the message will keep showing when reloading any page on Scanews.

Read more about the use of Cookies in the Tracking Technologies and Cookies section of our Privacy Policy.
